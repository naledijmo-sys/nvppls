How to Apply

Application Instructions

Review the job description

Read the full role requirements carefully. Ensure your experience matches at least 70% of the listed criteria before proceeding.

Prepare your documents

Updated CV (max 3 pages), a cover letter addressed to the hiring manager, and copies of qualifications.

Submit your application

Email your documents to careers@novapeople.com with the job title in the subject line, or click "Apply Now" on the listing.

Await confirmation

You will receive an automated acknowledgement within 24 hours. Shortlisted candidates are contacted within 14 business days.

Interview process

Two-stage process: a 30-minute screening call followed by a panel interview. Some roles include a practical assessment.
